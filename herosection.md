# Triode Studio – Premium Hero Section (Infinite Product Builder)

## Objective

Build a premium, modern, lightweight hero section that visually tells the story of how Triode Studio transforms ideas into finished digital products.

The animation should feel elegant, intentional, and premium—similar to Apple, Linear, Framer, Notion, and Vercel.

Do NOT use a laptop, phone, monitor, or other physical device.

The hero should focus on the creative process itself.

---

# Performance Requirements

Performance is the highest priority.

Build using:

- React
- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Framer Motion

Do NOT use:

- Three.js
- Canvas
- WebGL
- Videos
- GIFs
- Lottie
- Heavy SVG filters

Animate only:

- opacity
- transform
- scale
- rotate
- translate3d

Target:

- 60 FPS
- Lighthouse Performance >95
